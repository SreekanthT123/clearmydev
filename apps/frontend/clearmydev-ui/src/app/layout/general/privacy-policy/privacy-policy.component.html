Privacy Policy

ClearMyDev

Last updated: February 2026

Welcome to ClearMyDev. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our service.

1. What ClearMyDev Is

ClearMyDev is a developer productivity platform that helps users understand technical errors, logs, code changes, and incident descriptions by providing plain-English explanations powered by artificial intelligence.

2. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

When you sign in using Google authentication, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google account ID
  • Your profile image (if available)

We use this only to create and manage your account. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, or any other Google data.

B. Usage Information

We store limited usage data necessary to operate the platform:

  • Daily usage count (number of tool requests)
  • Account creation date
  • Last usage date

This helps enforce free-tier usage limits and prevent abuse.

C. Submitted Inputs (Important)

When you use ClearMyDev tools, you may paste:

  • Error messages
  • Logs
  • Configuration diffs
  • Incident descriptions
  • JSON payloads

These inputs are processed temporarily to generate explanations. We do NOT permanently store your debugging inputs on our servers.

3. Third-Party Processing (AI Provider)

To generate explanations, ClearMyDev sends submitted input to an AI service provider (OpenAI).

This means:

  • Your pasted content is transmitted to OpenAI for processing
  • The content is used only to generate a response

Please do not submit sensitive secrets such as:

  • Passwords
  • Private keys
  • Tokens
  • Proprietary company code

4. Cookies & Tracking

ClearMyDev does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics. We only store a secure authentication token in your browser to keep you logged in. No marketing tracking is performed.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account
  • Operate the service
  • Enforce usage limits
  • Prevent abuse
  • Improve platform reliability

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including secure authentication, protected API endpoints, and restricted database access. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

Account data is stored while your account exists. Debugging inputs are not permanently stored. You may request account deletion at any time.
